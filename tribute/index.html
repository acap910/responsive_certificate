Pelé

The man who revolutionized football

Pelé's Historic Journey

  • 1940 - Born Edson Arantes do Nascimento in Três Corações, Brazil
  • 1956 - Signed with Santos FC at age 15
  • 1958 - Became the youngest World Cup winner at 17 (Sweden)
  • 1962 - Won second World Cup with Brazil (Chile)
  • 1969 - Scored his legendary 1000th goal
  • 1970 - Led Brazil to third World Cup (Mexico), keeping Jules Rimet trophy
  • 1975 - Joined New York Cosmos, popularizing soccer in USA
  • 1999 - Named FIFA Co-Player of the Century
  • 2022 - Passed away at age 82 in São Paulo
"Pelé was the only footballer who surpassed the boundaries of logic." — Johan Cruyff
